# Become Daily
Your daily helper for personal growth through simple actions.
## Features
- Choose your identity: Active, Focused, Calm, Reader, Neat
- Complete daily actions to strengthen habits
- Track your streak and earn points
- Unlock achievement badges
- Get reminders at the right time
